Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know before you show up.

Do I need to buy a separate ticket to compete? +

No. Once you are inside the venue, all 6 tournament categories are completely free to enter. The gate fee covers everything. Arena Pass holders get priority seeding.

What's the difference between Gate, Arena, and Elite? +

Gate gets you in. Arena upgrades your experience — priority seeding, jersey, reserved seats, welcome pack. Elite is the full package: VIP lounge, pro meet & greet, front row, hoodie, afterparty.

Can I compete in more than one game category? +

Yes. You can register for multiple categories on-site subject to bracket capacity. Pre-registration via Arena or Elite Pass guarantees your spot before the day.

Do I need to bring my own controller or equipment? +

All tournament equipment is provided including consoles, monitors, controllers, and headsets. You may bring your own licensed peripheral but it must be approved by tournament officials on arrival.

Is this event for spectators only or for players? +

Both. Every ticket holder can watch every match from the arena floor. And every ticket holder can also compete — there's no separate player registration fee at the gate.

How do I pay and how do I get confirmation? +

Payment is processed via Paystack — supporting cards, bank transfer, USSD, and mobile money. You'll receive a confirmation email with your Player ID within minutes of purchase.

Can people travel from outside Lagos? +

Absolutely. We're already seeing pre-registration interest from Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, and beyond. Elite VIP includes priority check-in to help with travel logistics.

When does the venue and date get confirmed? +

The confirmed venue and exact date will be locked and announced publicly in 2026. All registered ticket holders will be notified first by email.
