Refund & Usage Policy

X Revive Gaming Events · Lagos, Nigeria Last updated: June 2026

About X Revive

X Revive is a gaming events brand based in Lagos, Nigeria. We organise physical esports and gaming tournament events where participants purchase tickets to compete or attend as spectators. Our events feature video game titles including EA FC 26, F1 25, and Mortal Kombat. Payments collected through our platform are solely for event ticket purchases.

Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change. Please read our refund terms carefully before purchasing your ticket.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refund requests made 7 or more days before the event date are eligible for a full refund.
  • Refund requests made 3 to 6 days before the event are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Refund requests made less than 48 hours before the event are not eligible for a refund.
  • No refunds will be issued for no-shows on the day of the event.

Event Cancellation or Postponement

If X Revive cancels or significantly postpones an event, all ticket holders will receive a full refund automatically, or the option to transfer their ticket to the rescheduled date. We will notify all ticket holders via email within 24 hours of any such decision.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to xrevivefest@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address used to purchase the ticket
  • Your payment reference or transaction ID
  • Reason for the refund request

Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 10 business days back to the original payment method.

  • By attending, you consent to your image or likeness being used in event coverage across our social media channels and marketing materials.

Payments
